Why start a watchbrand?

The watchmarket is oversaturated and difficult to get a new brand to compete and survive against the other big, renowned, brands.
So why this crazy idea? Let's get a deeper understanding.

(Psst: click the video to see how a mechanical movement work!)

DNA

The creator of the company comes from a Swiss watchmaker family dating 4 generations back.

The gift

The creator of the company received a pocket watch his great-great-grandfather created in 1899.

Reaction

The combination between the DNA and the gift planted a seed to create our own watchbrand and to honor the creators roots.

Prototypes

The first drawing

2022

The inspiration came from three different watches. From there we moved on to the first 2D render to get a better understanding of how it would look like.

2D render

2022

We put our first drawing into photoshop, following the measurements. Our first draft lacks the rounded inner bezel we want, bandwidth is to big and lack of angles on the lugs. So the work continues.

Practicing

2022

We have decided to use the ETA 2824-2 movement in our first watch and want to know everything about it. How it works, what parts are where, how to disassemble it and put it back togheter. In this picture you see our creator improve his already high mechanical skills to really understand what goes into our watches.

Final 2D render

2022

Our final 2D render. We are now moving on to CAD drawing and 3D printing a protoype in metal.

The first 3D model

2022

We want to proudly be able to say that we created the watch. Therefore we are learning CAD engineering and creating our own 3D models. This first draft lacks the angles on the lugs but we'll still 3D print it to see a real result. In that way we will know better what to alter for the second draft.
