Pricing

We are using a high quality, hand made, Swiss movement in 316L steel body. Which means costs. To keep the price low we decided to ship the clockwork movement to our factory in Shenzen and assemble the watches using robots there.
In that way you are getting a high quality watch at an affordable price that we are proud of creating!
We are yet at an extremely early stage so prices are to be announced but we are hoping to hit the sub 200$ market.
